Camille Belmin

PhD Candidate

Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK)

Camille Belmin, Dr.phil, is a researcher, curator, artist, and lecturer at IIASA and working independently. In her work on the intersection of art and science, she specializes in narratives and modalities of communication that shape socio-ecological transformation. She is a main member of SMA and has co-curated several art-science and collaborative projects such as exhibitions, workshop series, and art publications, while regularly teaching at various universities, such as the University of Applied Arts Vienna and University of Vienna. Camille Belmin is project co-lead; she would be one of the main curators and would be involved in organizational and administrative tasks.
